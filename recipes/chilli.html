Ash's Chilli

A delicious bowl of chilli.

Overview

Chilli is a dish that brings together beans, tomato and protein into a rich, spicy stew.

This version is vegan and quick to make. Serve it with steamed rice, warm tortillas or even a side of crusty bread for a satisfying meal that only gets better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 cup textured vegetable protein (TVP)
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 can of crushed tomato
  • 1 can of red kidney beans
  • 1 packet of burrito seasoning
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, soak the TVP in boiling water for 10 minutes and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic, sautéing until fragrant and lightly golden.
  4. Stir in the soaked TVP and cook for 2–3 minutes to develop flavour.
  5. Add the burrito seasoning and mix well to coat all ingredients evenly.
  6. Pour in the canned tomato and beans, stirring until combined.
  7. Simmer on low heat for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning, add salt or extra chilli to your liking.
  9. Serve hot with rice, bread or tortilla chips.
